## What is the SPC Portal?

The Security, Privacy & Compliance (SPC) Portal is a comprehensive suite of tools developed by the Genesys Security, Privacy & Compliance Center of Excellence team. This specialized platform helps organizations optimize and monitor their Genesys Cloud environment's security posture while ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations.
